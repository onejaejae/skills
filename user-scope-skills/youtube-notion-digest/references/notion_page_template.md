# Notion Page Template

Use this structure when creating or updating the final Notion page.

Keep the page dense with signal, easy to scan, and useful for decisions.

## Recommended page title

`[YouTube] <video title> - Decision Digest`

## Page outline

```md
# [YouTube] <video title> - Decision Digest

> Source
> - URL: <youtube url>
> - Channel: <channel name>
> - Published: <date>
> - Transcript quality: <High|Medium|Low>

## Decision Context
- Audience: <who this is for>
- Decision to improve: <what choice this doc should help>
- Horizon: <today|this sprint|this quarter>

## TL;DR
<2-4 lines. Focus on "what changed in our thinking".>

## Interview Signal Audit
- Strong signal: <well-supported ideas>
- Weak signal: <anecdotal/under-evidenced ideas>
- Missing evidence: <what the interview does not prove>

## Core Narrative
<3-6 bullets or short paragraphs that reconstruct the talk in a clearer order than the transcript>

## Section Timeline
| Time | Section | Summary |
|------|---------|---------|
| 00:00-03:20 | ... | ... |

## Key Points With Timestamps
### 1. <point>
- Evidence: <timestamp + grounded explanation>
- Meaning: <short implication>

### 2. <point>
- Evidence: <timestamp + grounded explanation>
- Meaning: <short implication>

## Non-Obvious Insights (Top 3-5)
### Insight 1: <short claim>
- Why this is non-obvious: <not a paraphrase>
- Evidence: <timestamp(s)>
- Boundary condition: <when this breaks>
- Action: <what to do next>

### Insight 2: <short claim>
- Why this is non-obvious: <not a paraphrase>
- Evidence: <timestamp(s)>
- Boundary condition: <when this breaks>
- Action: <what to do next>

## Decision Matrix
| Candidate move | Decision (Adopt/Watch/Reject) | Why | Preconditions | Owner type | Horizon |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| <move> | <decision> | <reason> | <what must be true> | <role> | <time> |

## 30-Day Experiments
| Experiment | Success metric | Risk | Cost | Stop rule |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| <test> | <metric> | <risk> | <cost> | <when to stop> |

## Blind Spots Or Counterpoints
- <missing perspective>
- <where confidence should be lower>

## Open Questions
- <question 1>
- <question 2>

## Transcript Quality Note
<what caption source was used and what limitations remain>
```

## Visual guidance

Apply structure before styling:

- use headings aggressively
- use a source callout near the top
- use a table only for timeline or comparison
- reserve callouts for caveats, not decoration
- add one decision callout only if it adds clarity

If you add stronger Notion styling later, do not change the meaning of the sections above.
